# LifeQuest - Core Rules Document (v0.1)

## Overview

LifeQuest is a non-violent, values-driven trading card game where players build meaningful communities, causes, or lives by strategically playing cards and managing shared resources. The game replaces combat with *progress-based competition*, emphasizing synergy, story, and thoughtful interaction.

## Objective

Accumulate **30 Life Points (LP)** by playing People, Locations, and Events that align with your chosen strategies and values. The first player to reach 30 LP at the end of a Winter phase wins.

---

## Components

- A 30-40 card deck per player
- 12 shared **Resource Tokens**, representing six core Values
- Life Point (LP) tracker or tokens
- Optional: Status tokens, reminder counters

---

## The Six Shared Resources (Values)

Each game begins with a pool of 12 Resource Tokens. The standard setup includes 2 of each:

1. **Growth** - Expansion, ambition, production
2. **Sustainability** - Balance, renewal, recycling
3. **Equity** - Fairness, inclusion, mutual aid
4. **Expression** - Creativity, combos, identity
5. **Learning** - Knowledge, planning, upgrades
6. **Solidarity** - Community, shared action

Resource tokens are claimed, exchanged, or spent during gameplay. Cards require specific resources to be played or activated.

---

## Card Types

There are three universal card types:

- **People** - Ongoing abilities or LP-generating characters. Some trigger effects based on Seasons or synergy.
- **Locations** - Persistent places that generate resources or amplify card effects.
- **Events** - One-time effects that reshape the board state or create momentum swings.

Cards may have *Tags* (e.g. Artist, Activist, Youth, Garden) that enable synergies.

---

## Game Setup

1. Place 12 Resource Tokens in the center of the table, using the standard 2x6 distribution or a custom league/event setup.
2. Each player draws 5 cards from their shuffled deck.
3. Randomly determine the first player.
4. Begin in **Spring of Year 1**.

---

## Turn Structure - The Four Seasons

Each game round consists of four phases, themed as **Seasons**:

### SPRING (Refresh & Growth)
- Draw 1 card.
- Claim up to 2 Resource Tokens from the shared pool **or** exchange 1 of your tokens for another from the pool.

### SUMMER (Action & Play)
- Play up to 2 cards from your hand (People, Locations, or Events).
- Spend Resource Tokens to pay card costs.
- Cards may generate immediate or ongoing effects.

### AUTUMN (Resolution & Combo)
- Resolve all card effects triggered by Summer plays.
- Optionally play 1 additional card (useful for chaining or reactive plays).
- Cards with "Autumn" triggers activate now.

### WINTER (Reflection & Scoring)
- Tally LP gains from all in-play cards.
- Resolve end-of-year triggers.
- Discard down to 5 cards if over hand limit.
- Return unspent tokens to the pool.
- Advance to the next Year.

---

## Winning the Game

- At the end of any **Winter phase**, if a player has **30 or more LP**, they win the game.
- If both players exceed 30 LP in the same Winter, the one with more total LP wins.
- In the case of a tie, play one additional Year.

---

## Custom Leagues & Events

- Local leagues may define their own starting Resource Pools (e.g., Duluth League begins with extra Expression and Sustainability tokens).
- Events can feature unique draft rules, shared decks, or community-built resource maps.

---

## Design Principles

- LifeQuest values collaboration, thoughtful planning, and strategic storytelling.
- Cards and resources are designed for accessibility and local flavor.
- There is no combat—only choices, consequences, and shared futures.
